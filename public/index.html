Hello and welcome to the UK REconomy Funding Database


The REconomy Funding database includes information on over 200 funds that provide grants, equity or other support to REconomy projects, Transition Enterprises and social enterprises working in the UK. Â 

This database has been developed to accompany our "Transition Enterprise Guide" which is packed full of information, inspiration and tips on how to start a REconomy project. A full description of terms used in the database, as well as detailed information on each funder type can be found in the guide.

For news, updates and inspiration from REconomists across the UK sign-up to our mailing list.

To access the database click the "start now" button.

The search fields at the top of the page can be used to narrow the list down to find specific funders, funding types or funding available in your location. This is the second edition of the REconomy Funding Database which includes information gathered in May 2017.

We love to hear your REconomy stories, share them with us via Facebook and Twitter

If you have any comments, suggestions or know of any funds or funders that you would like to share please contact Transition Network or tweet us at @REconomyProject
